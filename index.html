A Deep Learning Method for Document Shadow Removal under Mask Supervision with Sobel Prior

Jiarui Li

The 39th Annual AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence(AAAI 2025) Undergraduate Consortium

Philadelphia, USA. February, 2025.

21 undergraduate students around the world

[PDF]

The removal of document shadows is of great importance for the readability of documents. To address the issue of document shadow removal, we propose a method based on the UNET framework. We have incorporated a MaskNet for determining the position of the mask and a FusionNet for feature fusion. Additionally, we utilize the prior knowledge of Sobel Prior to assist in the more effective removal of shadows.

Dynamic Parallel Traction Theoretical Model for the Application and Validation in Femoral Neck Fractures

Jiarui Li, Kunyue Xing, Wenzhuo Wang, Li Sun, Linyuan Xue, Jiyao Xing, Xiaolin Wu, Dongming Xing

Journal of Orthopaedics

[PDF] [Link]

The misalignment of the force line in bone traction often leads to various complications for patients. To address this issue, we have proposed a dynamic parallel traction algorithm that can resolve the problem of force line misalignment through dynamic angle adjustment. Furthermore, its effectiveness has been verified through finite element analysis. We have now completed the production of the prototype.
