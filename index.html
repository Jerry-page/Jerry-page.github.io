Research

My research work and interests include computer vision, machine vision especially for robots, computational imaging, medical robots and so on. Beblow are the projects that related to these areas.

Document Shadow Removal

We proposed a new architecture for document shadow removal, and this architecture combines with mask estimation, feature fusion based on UNet which shows state-of-the-art on SD7K dataset. Thanks to Dr. Zeyu's advice and cooperation.

Using the relative motions of stars we can accurately estimate the date of origin of historical astronomical images.
